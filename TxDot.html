TXDoT Digital Newsroom

User and market research to help the Texas Department of Transportation prepare for the creation of a digital newsroom
Client: Texas Department of Transportation

Summary

Role: Researcher
Class: Usability (Eric Nordquist, Clinical Associate Professor)
Date: August - December 2018

Objective

Help TXDoT figure out the best way to approach the creatino of a digital newsroom that caters to both the public and the press, making them a primary source for news and information about traffic and travel in Texas.

Final Recommendations

Based on my findings from the study, I recommended that TXDoT work towards a newsroom through damage control first and content creation second, while pursuing usability improvements on their existing website in the background.
