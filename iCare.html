iCare

A provider-facing mobile solution that improves care coordination for HIV and substance use treatment
Client: Dell Medical School Department of Psychiatry

Summary

Team:
Diana Mendoza (UX Designer);
Erin Finley (UX Designer);

As part of a UX team in the Healthcare Experience Lab at the University of Texas at Austin's School of Information, I was assigned to work on a healthcare system for clinicians to better provide care to their patients, communicate with other clinics and providers about the patients' treatment plans, and conduct referrals to other clinics.
