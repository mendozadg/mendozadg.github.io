About Me

I am a user experience designer.

I am a Master's student at UT-Austin focusing on user experience design & research. My undergraduate career was in Pre-Columbian archaeology, visual art, and French.

My professional UX experience includes:

  • a graduate assistantship at my school's Healthcare Experience Lab, where I am designing a healthcare provider-facing system in collaboration with the Dell Medical School's psychiatry department;
  • and a contract consultant role for a Houston-based oil and gas company, where I am designing the user experience for their SpeedWise suite of products.
